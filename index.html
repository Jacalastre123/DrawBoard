Welcome to DrawBoard

Here is the guide for each of the functions:

  • Colour: Colour of shape/line
  • Eraser: Erase lines
  • Thickness: Change thickness of line
  • Image: Load a draggable image
  • Clear Board/Delete Image/Text: Clear the whole board/delete specific image or text (Get the specific image/text by clicking it afterwards)
  • Text: Creates draggable text
  • Shapes: Make a Square or Circle
  • Utilities: Make Borders, darken the screen or save as jpg
  • Text to Speech: To speak selected text (Select by clicking on the text afterwards)
  • Clock Widget: Makes a draggable clock widget of the time live.
